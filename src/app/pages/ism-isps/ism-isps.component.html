ISM / ISPS

The ISM is a documented safety management system, which formalizes procedures that a well-run yacht would adopt. The Code promotes a safety culture and greatly assists the yachtâ€™s crew to maintain and constantly improve standards of operation. Compliance to this Code is mandatory for commercial vessels over 500GT. The advantage of using a documented system is that it provides records and reports, which are audited by third parties, and demonstrates that the vessel is run correctly and to a high standard. ISPS code applies to port facilities serving ships on international voyages, to all passenger ships on international voyages, and to all other ships over 500 GT on international voyages. Therefore, large yachts over 500 GT will need to comply if they operate commercially.

Should the owner decide to implement full ISM/ISPS with OBY, the service will be provided at a separate rate.

Even if the yacht is not subject to ISM, we will still operate within the parameters of ISM to ensure the highest standard of safety and performance.
