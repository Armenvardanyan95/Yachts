Crew Management

OBY will assist the owner in developing efficient payroll structures. We recruit crew based on professionally developed crew contracts that minimize the owner’s liability. Prior to organizing potential crew interviews with the owner, we will verify the qualifications, check references and ensure that the candidates are eligible for the yacht’s flag manning requirements. We will also take care of all the travel and visa arrangements for the crew.

During our years as yacht management company we have learned that the best way of retaining qualified crew is offering them relevant training. This also enhances crew’s efficiency in emergency situations which ultimately benefits the yacht.
