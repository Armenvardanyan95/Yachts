Procurement & Other Yacht Services

OBY will work closely with the Captain and the crew to obtain necessary spares and parts. We DO NOT CHARGE any hidden fees! All the discounts obtained from different vendors (satellite providers, engineering, deck and interior suppliers, etc.) are passed directly to the yacht. OBY will keep all the yacht registration and other documents up to date. We will ensure that all the class and flag state surveys are up to date and organize all the necessary inspections and surveys.
