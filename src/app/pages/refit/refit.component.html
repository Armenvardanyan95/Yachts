Operational Management

Refit

OBY will work closely with the captain and the crew to determine necessary annual repairs. These works will be organized in such a manner so that not to affect the owner’s time onboard or the charters. Recognizing many owners’ complaints about uncontrollable shipyard periods, we will make this process as smooth as possible: from the very first stage of submitting a detailed estimate to the owner, monitoring the spending and work progress on a weekly basis by qualified project managers, informing the owner and seeking approval for any unexpected expenses, to providing a detailed report about the performed works, etc. We will leverage our existing relationships with the shipyards and subcontractors around the world to ensure that all the possible savings are passed to the yachts.
